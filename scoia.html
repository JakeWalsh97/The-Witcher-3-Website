The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Scoia'tael

Scoia'tael

The Scoia'tael is a flexible and really interesting deck that requires a great deal of skill to play properly. Where the Nilfgaard and the Realms are truly straightforward and the Monsters are pure brute force comprised of monsters, the Squirrels focus on combining debuffs that affect the enemy units like the Weather cards, along with a moderately strong assortment of units.

Units with Agility can be deployed in either the Close or Ranged combat rows, giving you tactical flexibility: Field a debuff against a strong enemy unit set, then counter with your own units in unaffected rows. Another advantage of the deck is that Geralt get to choose who plays first. While the AI usually chooses to be the first to play its cards, it's usually more advantageous to let the opponent play when you turn.

Leader Cards - Francesca Findabair

Pick a Biting Frost card from your deck and play it instantly.

Draw an extra card at the beginning of the battle.

Doubles the strength of all your Ranged Combat units

Destroy your enemy's strongest Close Combat unit(s) if the combined strength is 10 or more.
