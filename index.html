The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Official Website

Welcome

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a story-driven, next-generation open world role-playing game, set in a visually stunning fantasy universe, full of meaningful choices and impactful consequences. In The Witcher, you are a professional monster hunter, tasked with finding a child of prophecy in a vast open world rich with merchant cities, dangerous mountain passes, and forgotten caverns to explore.


Characters

There are hundreds of characters within the Witcher world, all with unique personalities, abilities and stories to tell. Some of the main characters include Geralt of Rivia, Yennefer of Vengerberg, Triss Merigold and Ciri. There are also lots of villainous characters such as Imlerith and Eredin who are part of the Wild Hunt.


Creatures

The realm is filled with many creatures both magical and dangerous. The majority of creatures are dangerous and will act aggressively towards Geralt when he approaches. Some of the mystical creatures include Vampires, Wraiths, Werewolves and Griffins. Geralt can use his silver sword to attack non-humans and his steel sword against humans.

Gwent

Gwent is a card game played by many innkeepers and peasants around the land. The game is turn based and the highest score on the board wins each round. The game is divided into 4 factions: Northern Realms, Nilfgaardian Empire, Scoiatael and Monsters. Unique hero cards are most valuable as they cannot be countered in the game.
