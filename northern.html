The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Northern Realms

Northern Realms

The Northern Realms deck has the potential ability to place unit cards with the Tight Bond ability (each affected unit has its strength increased by 100%) in each of the three rows, perhaps all in one round depending on the cards the player draws, which can make a very small number of cards get a very massive score if you lay them down together, which you can then boost further with the Commander's Horn.

Depending on the draw, players can receive as many as 15 cards from their deck {3 spy cards, opponent uses 3 spy cards which are reclaimed with decoys, mysterious elf spy card, northern realms ability after winning one round}. The main weaknesses of this deck are: Great susceptibility to Weather cards and Scorch cards.

Leader Cards - Foltest

Pick an Impenetrable Fog card from your deck and play it instantly.

Clear any weather effects (on both sides) currently in play.

Doubles the strength of all Siege units.


Destroy opponent's strongest Siege units if the strength of that row is 10 or higher.
