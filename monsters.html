The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Monsters

Monsters

The Monsters are led by wild hunt characters who are the leaders of this deck. The Monsters faction ability is to keep one random unit card after each round on the battlefield, and they by far have the largest number of Muster cards. They also however have no Spy or Medic cards. Unlike the setup of your decks for the Northern Realms or Nilfgaardian Empire, where you usually want only the 22 best units you have for the deck, for Monsters you want to fill your deck with as many of the Muster cards as you can get your hands on.

Have plenty of Clear Weather cards handy to prevent this, and remember that the Muster swarming effect only happens once; when picking your two cards to swap at the initial setup try to get the most mixed set of Muster card types you can, so you can do Muster swarms several times and not get quickly burned out of your strength in the first round.

Leader Cards - Eredin

Pick any weather card from your deck and play it instantly.


Double the strength of all your Close Combat units (unless Commander's horn in use)

Discard 2 cards and draw 1 card of your choice from your deck.


Restore a card from your discard pile to your hand.
