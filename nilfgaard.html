The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Nilfgaardian Empire

Nilfgaardian Empire

The Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck must be built from scratch by acquiring cards.

The deck consists of a total of 38 available Gwent cards. In general, the Nilfgaard card set consists primarily of strong stand-alone cards; a few standard units have point values of 10. However they are quite limited in the number of cards that can strengthen each other; they do have a few with Tight Bond but apart from the Impera Brigade Guard quartet those cards are significantly weaker than the Tight Bond ones found in the Northern Realms Gwent deck.

Like the Northern Realms deck there are three standard spy cards but the point values for the Nilfgaard variants are higher.
